# 250PROUD Brand & Creative Guidelines

## Core Directive: "No AI Slop"
All creative output (copywriting, design, code architecture, and communication) must feel distinctly **human, premium, and authentic**. 
- Avoid generic AI phrasing, predictable repetitive structures, or over-wrought prose.
- The brand is built on grit, freedom, and pride. It must sound like it was written and built by passionate people who care deeply about the 250th anniversary of the United States.
- The quality of the final product must reflect a high-end, bespoke level of craftsmanship. It should never look or feel like a cut-and-paste output.
- Every deliverable is a reflection of the brand's integrity. Be protective of the brand's image. Our audience must immediately sense the genuine human effort and expertise behind the project.
