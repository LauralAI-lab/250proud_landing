2. The "Soul & Cadence" Intake Architecture (Anti-AI-Slop Engine)
Unlike generic AI wrappers, AB requires a 3-step voice calibration protocol before deploying sub-agents:

Step A: Raw Cadence Harvesting
The onboarding wizard collects 3 to 5 samples of the user's authentic communication (unedited listing descriptions, personal client emails, or a 60-second raw voice memo answering "How do you explain the local market over coffee?").

Step B: Stylistic DNA Extraction (Gemini 1.5 Pro)
Gemini 1.5 Pro analyzes the input across four critical dimensions to generate a persistent Stylistic DNA Matrix:
• Sentence Structure & Rhythm: Fragment usage, cadence length, and conversational flow.
• Regional Lexicon: Local terminology, warmth index, and vocabulary preferences.
• Tone Anchor: Direct/analytical, warm Southern hospitality, or high-end luxury formal.
• Banned Slop Filter: Hard-coded rejection of generic AI buzzwords (e.g., delve, nestled, tapestry, game-changer, seamless, beacon, testament).

Step C: System Rule Enforcement
The Stylistic DNA Matrix is injected into the master system prompt governing all sub-agents. Every output is audited against the user's matrix prior to rendering.
